# Privacy Rules

Treat the repository and its history as permanently public.

Never commit:

- Salary, income, or net worth
- Immigration status
- Full address or phone number
- Employer internal details
- Confidential work details
- Legal plans
- Relationship history
- Political views
- Private family information

Public-safe links may be added later:

- LinkedIn
- GitHub
- An optional email address intended for public contact

When uncertain, leave the information out until it has been reviewed.
